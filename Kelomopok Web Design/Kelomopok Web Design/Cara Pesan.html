Food websites are like one-stop shops. Even though the contents are just food,
Starting from Sulawesi, Kalimantan, Javanese food. to food delivery service providers. Ordering food and drinks at SaNusa is as easy as drinking through a straw. Here's how to order food via the website

Start by selecting the menu on the SaNusa Website www.SaNusa.com does not yet support the restaurant search feature

Select the menu you want to order on the restaurant profile page

Each menu selected will be added to the basket. To access your basket, click on the Basket icon in the top right corner, to the right of your profile photo.

In the Basket menu, we can add or reduce quantities and give special notes to the restaurant for each menu ordered.

Click the Continue checkout button under the basket menu to continue ordering

On the order checkout page, enter the details of the delivery destination address. We can also change the delivery point by clicking the Change Address button

On the checkout page, we can also use the available promos by clicking Use on the desired voucher. Then click Message to continue.

Before the order is sent to the restaurant, we will be asked to check once again all the details, such as: delivery address, order summary, promotions used, and price details.

Your order has been sent to the restaurant after this stage. Once confirmed, the restaurant will immediately prepare the order.
